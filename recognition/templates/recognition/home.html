Camera Guidelines for Attendance Marking

  • Ensure Proper Lighting: Make sure you are in a well-lit area to enhance the visibility of your face.
  • Keep Your Face Centered: Position your face in the center of the camera frame for accurate recognition.
  • Use Thumbs Up and Blink: Raise your thumb and blink during the attendance process. This helps confirm your presence and prevents proxy attendance.
  • Maintain a Neutral Expression: Avoid excessive facial movements to ensure a clear capture of your facial features.
  • Stay Still: Remain still while the camera captures your image to improve recognition accuracy.
